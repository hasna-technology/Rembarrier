LEADERSHIP SEARCH
Critical evaluation of position
We help companies to evaluate the Value of the position in comparison with other key positions to determine the authority, responsibility, structure, title and compensation. This helps to reduce the subjectivity and makes the Systems fair and transparent.
Domain expertise of consultants
Our search consultants have served various industries in leadership positions and have the requisite domain expertise to evaluate the skill sets of the candidates.
International sourcing of candidates
We have a strong research team to source leaders of different nationality with strong international experience.
Referenced through 360 deg feedback
We do 360deg reference check of shortlisted candidates which helps in objective evaluation of leadership qualities of the candidates.
Technical and social skills evaluated scientifically through SLT
It is common practice that the technical skills of a person is often evaluated if he can do a particular job and is offered the job. However social skills are equally if not more important for leadership position.
Sounding board approach
After the candidate joins the organization we provide sounding board assistance to the candidate as well as the client for a period or as agreed to help the candidate fitment into the organization.
Interim management
We also help organizations to find leadership candidates for short term contract to fill up for any specific need. The candidates will need a different mindset to accept these roles.
Mentoring and coaching
We have on board trained and certified coaches of different levels who can help leaders to polish their skills and achieve their full potential. They often help leaders to solve complex issues by bringing in the required insight and clarity.
